Training

Internal Training & Curriculum Alignment

Most manufacturers have some form of internal training — onboarding checklists, informal mentoring, occasional toolbox talks. Few have training that is documented, sequenced, and aligned to the standards their customers or regulators actually care about. The gap between what employees are taught and what AWS, OSHA, or NCCER require is often larger than anyone realizes until an audit makes it obvious.

This service reviews your existing training materials and practices against applicable standards, identifies the gaps, and produces a roadmap to close them — or builds the aligned curriculum from scratch. The same work done for publishers and CTE programs, applied to your operation.
